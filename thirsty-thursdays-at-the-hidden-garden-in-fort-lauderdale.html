Thirsty Thursdays at The Hidden Garden In Fort Lauderdale

Fort Lauderdale, FL - Get ready for the ultimate Thursday night experience at The Hidden Garden in Fort Lauderdale. The night, dubbed Thirsty Thursday, is aimed at college students and is set to be a wild ride in Wonderland!

Thirsty Thursday will feature $99 bottle specials all night long, giving party-goers the perfect excuse to let loose and enjoy the night. In addition, Alice's Watermelon Lemonade will be available at a special price of just $10, providing a refreshing and delicious drink option.

For the early birds, the Hidden Garden has something special in store. Between 8 PM and 10 PM, we're offering two for one drink specials, giving you double the fun for half the price. And, for those who are looking for an after-party, we've got you covered with a reverse happy hour from 12 AM to 2 AM where the bar staff will offer $5 premium drafts, $7 premium cocktails

And $9 premium wines until the last song of the night!

For those marking a special occasion, the Mad Hatters Hat Trick Bottle Special is the perfect choice. This VIP experience comes with a reserved table and three premium bottles - Tito’s Vodka, Milagro Tequila, and Bacardi Rum - all for just $450. And for those who want to take their celebration to the next level, the Long Island Bottle Special is now available. This new offering includes premium Vodka, Rum, Tequila, and Gin bottles, paired with a variety of mixers, making it a fitting choice for an “adult tea party” in wonderland.


The Hidden Garden is proud to announce the launch of a brand new ITB (In the Biz) program for 2023! This exciting new initiative offers a 50% discount on all bar tabs to anyone who works in the industry, making it the perfect spot to relax, unwind, and mingle with your peers. To take advantage of this offer, simply bring a business card, or pay stub, or come in uniform to the bar to enjoy the savings! This new program is a testament to the Hidden Garden's commitment to supporting those who work in the hospitality industry. So, whether you're a bartender, server, line cook, host, or any of the other vital team members that make this business work, come enjoy some well-deserved savings and recognition.

 

Thursdays at Hidden Garden promise to be an electrifying experience, with a never-ending celebration featuring top-notch drinks, pulse-pumping music, and an unbeatable atmosphere. It's the ultimate destination for those seeking to unwind and have a good time. Whether you're looking to dance the night away or simply socialize with friends, The Hidden Garden on Thursdays is the place to be.


For more information, please visit www.TheHiddenGardenLasOlas.com or call 754-200-6344.

ABOUT PDKN RESTAURANT GROUP

PDKN Restaurant Group was founded by former Miami Dolphins player Kim Bokamper and his three partners, P.J. Kavanagh, Damon DeSantis, and Noel Cullen in 2008. Following Bokamper’s successful sports and broadcasting career he was approached by current partner Kavanagh about furthering his brand through a restaurant. The first Bokamper’s Sports Bar & Grill opened in 2008 in Plantation, Florida. 

Today PDKN owns three Bokamper’s Sports Bar and Grill locations in Miramar, Plantation, and off the Intracoastal waterways of Fort Lauderdale, Florida, all dedicated to providing exceptional service, great food, and an unparalleled atmosphere for friends, families, and sports fans of all ages. PDKN Restaurant Group also owns and operates Bo’s Beach, a casual beach restaurant featuring fresh seafood and stunning ocean views on Fort Lauderdale Beach; La Playa Dayclub, a Tulum-inspired open-air, house music day club located just steps away from the Atlantic Ocean shoreline in Fort Lauderdale, Bo’s Pub, a stylish sports bar located on the first floor at 1309 E. Las Olas Boulevard; The Balcony Bar, Fort Lauderdale’s only rooftop bar with a direct view of Las Olas, easily accessible on the second floor at 1309 E. Las Olas Blvd.; and PDKN’s latest creation The Hidden Garden, Fort Lauderdale’s newest upscale rooftop lounge and bar concept, located on the second floor at 1309 E. Las Olas Boulevard.

ABOUT THE HIDDEN GARDEN.

The Hidden Garden, a PDKN Restaurant Group concept, is Fort Lauderdale’s newest upscale Rooftop Bar and Lounge. From the moment patrons arrive at The Hidden Garden they are transported to another world. Lush foliage and tropical greenery welcome each guest as they enter, while surprise theme activations hidden throughout the rooftop are sure to delight our more curious and adventurous visitors.
