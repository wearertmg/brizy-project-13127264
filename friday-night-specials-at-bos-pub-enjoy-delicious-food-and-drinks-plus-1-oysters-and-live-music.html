Friday Night Specials at Bo's Pub: Enjoy Delicious Food and Drinks Plus $1 Oysters and Live Music!

Fort Lauderdale, FL - This Friday, and every Friday, come to Bo’s Pub on Las Olas in Fort Lauderdale for a night of fun and great deals. Beginning at 12 p.m. guests receive ½ off their pub-style appetizers and beverages including premium liquor, signature cocktails, wine, craft beers, and seltzers. Additionally, from 5 p.m. - 7 p.m., guests can also enjoy $1 oysters.  

 

These gastro-pub-style appetizers are available from 12 p.m. - 7 p.m. all for $10 and under!

 

  • Crispy Crab & Conch Fritters w/ arugula, aioli, and burnt lemon vinaigrette
  • Pork Belly Lettuce Wraps w/ char-siu glaze, carrots, cucumber, radish, red pepper, Boston lettuce, and cilantro
  • The Balcony Calamari w/ sweet chili & roasted garlic
  • Tempura Artichoke Hearts w/ aioli & grilled lime
  • Chilled Shrimp & Gazpacho w/ avocado and heirloom tomatoes
  • Bo’s Pub Wings w/ our signature sauce
  • Crab  & Artichoke Dip w/ crostini
  • Lollipop Lamb Chops w/ artichoke crust & demi-glace
  • Sesame Seed Ahi Tuna w/ soy, chives, and ginger lime slaw

 

Friday nights at Bo’s Pub are not only for great deals on food and beverages, but also to catch live entertainment. Every Friday night on #stage1309 Bo's features a different live band playing a variety of genres from Jazz, Pop, Soul, and Rock performed by award-winning artists, vibrant young bands, and even the famous Tony Wilson who was deemed “The Young James Brown” by the Godfather of Soul himself.  One of the biggest draws to #Stage1309 at Bo’s Pub is the Dueling Piano Show which happens on the first Friday of every month and the second saturday of every month.  This live, interactive, musical experience has guests singing and dancing to the most popular songs of the last few decades with two amazing hosts who ensure an incredible atmosphere.

ABOUT BO’S PUB.

Bo’s Pub, a PDKN Restaurant Group concept, is your favorite classic American sports bar, elevated. 

Located in the heart of Fort Lauderdale’s most active entertainment and fine dining district, Las Olas Boulevard, and conveniently nestled directly underneath another of PDKN Restaurant Group’s smash hits, and the only 2nd story balcony bar with a direct view of Las Olas, The Balcony! Bo’s Pub delights guests with timeless American dishes accompanied by an extensive wine menu, and offers a welcoming atmosphere accented with gas lamps, pub signs, and a giant 12-foot LED screen.

For more information, follow us on socials @BosPubLasOlas, visit our website at www.BosPubLasOlas.com or call us at (754) 200-6344.
