PDKN Restaurant Group Offers Chance to Win Dream Trip to Ireland for Two by Supporting Tullamore D.E.W.!

Fort Lauderdale, FL – March is the month of St. Patrick's Day, and PDKN Restaurant Group is celebrating the occasion in a big way. The restaurant group is proud to announce a chance to win their annual dream trip to Ireland for two, including a 6-night stay in Ireland, airfare, a Tullamore D.E.W. distillery tour, and a Guinness brewery tour. The best part? All you have to do to enter the contest is purchase one of the following: A D.E.W. and A Brew, which includes a Tullamore D.E.W. shot and a Guinness, a Tullamore D.E.W. Mule, or a green tea shot.

The contest is being offered at several locations, including Bokamper’s Plantation, Bokamper’s Fort Lauderdale, Bo’s Beach Fort Lauderdale Beach, Bo’s Pub Fort Lauderdale, The Balcony Fort Lauderdale, and La Playa Dayclub Fort Lauderdale Beach.

But that's not all! From Friday, March 10 through Sunday, March 19, the restaurant group will also be running a Corned Beef & Cabbage promotion for $19 at several locations, including Bokamper’s Plantation, Bokamper’s Fort Lauderdale, and Bo’s Pub. Furthermore, visitors can partake in the ultimate St. Patrick's Day Celebration at Bo's Pub and The Balcony on Las Olas on Friday, March 17th.

"PDKN Restaurant Group is thrilled to offer our customers the chance to win an unforgettable trip to Ireland," said Emily Dall, Operations Director of PDKN Restaurant Group.. "We wanted to celebrate St. Patrick's Day in a big way and what better way to do that than by offering our customers a chance to experience the beauty and magic of Ireland? We hope our customers will join us in supporting D.E.W. and enter the contest for a chance to win this amazing trip."

The contest will run from March 1 to March 31, and the winner will be selected in a random drawing in early April. For more information about the contest and the promotional events, visit the website for the associated restaurant below.

  • Bokamper’s Plantation
  • Bokamper’s Fort Lauderdale
  • Bo’s Beach Fort Lauderdale Beach
  • Bo’s Pub Fort Lauderdale
  • The Balcony Fort Lauderdale
  • La Playa Dayclub Fort Lauderdale Beach

If you're eager to fully immerse yourself in the Irish spirit, then join PDKN for an unforgettable experience. Witness the live performance of the NYPD Pipes & Drums of the Emerald Society at Bo's Beach on March 10th at 7pm or Bokamper’s Fort Lauderdale March 11th at 7pm.

ABOUT PDKN RESTAURANT GROUP

PDKN Restaurant Group was founded by former Miami Dolphins player Kim Bokamper and his three partners, P.J. Kavanagh, Damon DeSantis, and Noel Cullen in 2008. Following Bokamper’s successful sports and broadcasting career he was approached by current partner Kavanagh about furthering his brand through a restaurant. The first Bokamper’s Sports Bar & Grill opened in 2008 in Plantation, Florida. 

Today PDKN owns three Bokamper’s Sports Bar and Grill locations in Miramar, Plantation, and off the Intracoastal waterways of Fort Lauderdale, Florida, all dedicated to providing exceptional service, great food, and an unparalleled atmosphere for friends, families, and sports fans of all ages. PDKN Restaurant Group also owns and operates Bo’s Beach, a casual beach restaurant featuring fresh seafood and stunning ocean views on Fort Lauderdale Beach; La Playa Dayclub, a Tulum-inspired open-air, house music day club located just steps away from the Atlantic Ocean shoreline in Fort Lauderdale, Bo’s Pub, a stylish sports bar located on the first floor at 1309 E. Las Olas Boulevard; The Balcony Bar, Fort Lauderdale’s only rooftop bar with a direct view of Las Olas, easily accessible on the second floor at 1309 E. Las Olas Blvd.; and PDKN’s latest creation The Hidden Garden, Fort Lauderdale’s newest upscale rooftop lounge and bar concept, located on the second floor at 1309 E. Las Olas Boulevard.

About Tullamore D.E.W.

Tullamore D.E.W. is a brand of Irish whiskey originally produced in Tullamore, County Offaly, Ireland,. Tullamore D.E.W. is known for its smooth and approachable flavor profile, and it is a blend of three types of Irish whiskey: pot still, malt, and grain whiskey. It is often enjoyed neat, on the rocks, or in a variety of cocktails. Tullamore D.E.W. is a popular brand of Irish whiskey, and it has won numerous awards for its quality and taste. For more information on Tullamore D.E.W. please visit https://www.tullamoredew.com/.
