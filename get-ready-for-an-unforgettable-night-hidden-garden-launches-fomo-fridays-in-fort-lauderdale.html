Get Ready for an Unforgettable Night: Hidden Garden Launches FOMO Fridays in Fort Lauderdale

Fort Lauderdale - The Hidden Garden in Fort Lauderdale is thrilled to announce the launch of FOMO Fridays, a new and exciting nightlife experience in the heart of downtown Fort Lauderdale. Whether you're looking to dance the night away, enjoy a delicious cocktail, or simply spend some quality time with friends, FOMO Fridays has something for everyone.

The night starts off with a special "Beat the Clock" promotion from 8 PM to 11 PM, giving you the chance to try a variety of fun Alice in Wonderland themed cocktails. At 8 PM, you can enjoy Alice's Watermelon Lemonade (made with Tito's Vodka) for just $8, followed by The Mad Hatter's Cocorita (made with Milagro) at 9 PM for $9, Caterpillar's Kiwi Potion (made with Bacardi) at 10 PM for $10, and finally, the Down the Rabbit Hole Martini (made with Stoli Vodka) at 11 PM for $11.

In addition to these fun drinks, Hidden Garden is also offering $5 shot specials all night and $99 bottle specials all night, making it the perfect place to grab a drink and celebrate the weekend with friends.


For those celebrating something special, the Mad Hatter’s Hat Trick Bottle Special is the perfect choice. This VIP experience comes with a reserved table and three premium beverages - Tito’s Vodka, Milagro Tequila, and Bacardi Rum - all for just $450. And for those who want to take their celebration to the next level, the Long Island Bottle Special is now available. This new offering includes premium Vodka, Rum, Tequila, and Gin bottles, paired with a variety of mixers, making it a fitting choice for an “adult tea party” in wonderland.


New in 2023 is the highly anticipated ITB (In the Biz) program at the Hidden Garden. This exciting new initiative offers a 50% discount on all bar tabs to anyone who works in the industry, making it the perfect spot to relax, unwind, and mingle with your peers. To take advantage of this offer, simply bring a business card, or pay stub, or come in uniform to the bar to enjoy the savings! This new program is a testament to the Hidden Garden's commitment to supporting those who work in the hospitality industry. So, whether you're a bartender, server, line cook, host, or any of the other vital team members that make this business work, come enjoy some well-deserved savings and recognition.

FOMO Fridays will feature a different fun theme each week, so you don't want to miss out! Whether you're looking for a wild night out with friends or a laid-back evening with a loved one, the Hidden Garden is the place to be. So gather your crew, and make plans to join us for a night of adventure, drinks, and good times.

ABOUT PDKN RESTAURANT GROUP

PDKN Restaurant Group was founded by former Miami Dolphins player Kim Bokamper and his three partners, P.J. Kavanagh, Damon DeSantis, and Noel Cullen in 2008. Following Bokamper’s successful sports and broadcasting career he was approached by current partner Kavanagh about furthering his brand through a restaurant. The first Bokamper’s Sports Bar & Grill opened in 2008 in Plantation, Florida. 

Today PDKN owns three Bokamper’s Sports Bar and Grill locations in Miramar, Plantation, and off the Intracoastal waterways of Fort Lauderdale, Florida, all dedicated to providing exceptional service, great food, and an unparalleled atmosphere for friends, families, and sports fans of all ages. PDKN Restaurant Group also owns and operates Bo’s Beach, a casual beach restaurant featuring fresh seafood and stunning ocean views on Fort Lauderdale Beach; La Playa Dayclub, a Tulum-inspired open-air, house music day club located just steps away from the Atlantic Ocean shoreline in Fort Lauderdale, Bo’s Pub, a stylish sports bar located on the first floor at 1309 E. Las Olas Boulevard; The Balcony Bar, Fort Lauderdale’s only rooftop bar with a direct view of Las Olas, easily accessible on the second floor at 1309 E. Las Olas Blvd.; and PDKN’s latest creation The Hidden Garden, Fort Lauderdale’s newest upscale rooftop lounge and bar concept, located on the second floor at 1309 E. Las Olas Boulevard.

ABOUT THE HIDDEN GARDEN.

The Hidden Garden, a PDKN Restaurant Group concept, is Fort Lauderdale’s newest upscale Rooftop Bar and Lounge. From the moment patrons arrive at The Hidden Garden they are transported to another world. Lush foliage and tropical greenery welcome each guest as they enter, while surprise theme activations hidden throughout the rooftop are sure to delight our more curious and adventurous visitors.
